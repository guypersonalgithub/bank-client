Hello!
This website is being hosted under netlify for its frontend, and Heroku for its backend.
The free tier of Heroku is relatively limited, and thus, upon a certain threshold of user inactivity, the VM instance where the backend is being hosted, falls asleep, until new user requests wake it up.
Because of that, user experience may slightly suffer from inconviniences. However, once the VM instance boots up, everything will resume to work as usual.
